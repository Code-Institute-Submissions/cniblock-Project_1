Sarah Williamson

Sarah Williamson has been part of Zebra's senior leadership team since 2018, first as Global Managing Partner, and since 2020, as the firm’s Senior Partner.

During that time she has led the firm through a period of significant change in the legal industry and the global business landscape, winning recognition in 2018 as “Law Firm leader of the Year” in the British Legal Awards. Driving innovation and change and embedding a growth mindset are the hallmarks of her approach.
